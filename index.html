關於我們 About Us

投資哲學與跨境資本配置 Investment Philosophy & Capital Allocation

晉昌國際（Jin Chang Development Group）作為一家跨境私募股權與資產投資機構，專注於在全球多個司法管轄區內尋求具備結構性調整空間的資產與企業。我們不對一般公眾提供零售金融或地產仲介服務，而是做為機構合作夥伴在跨境資本配置、債務重組及股權注資時的戰略後盾。

我們堅信「資本保全與價值重塑」的雙重法則。在經濟週期波動中，我們透過深度估值與架構重整，協助面臨流動性瓶頸或治理瓶頸的高價值資產重新恢復生機，進而實現長期、穩健的資本增長。

Established on the principles of capital preservation and structural agility, Jin Chang Development Group operates as a premier cross-border asset investment and private equity institution. We do not provide retail brokerage or general real estate sales to the public. Instead, we act as a strategic advisor for institutional partners in managing multi-jurisdictional capital configuration.

Our focus lies in unlocking intrinsic value from mispriced corporate assets and distressed holdings, restructuring debt profiles, and delivering absolute risk-adjusted returns to our partners through deep operational restructuring and capital enhancement.

Pillar 01

資本安全防護 Capital Preservation

透過跨司法管轄區的信託與法律結構化設計，將地緣政治與匯率風險降至最低，首要確保合夥人核心資產的安全性。 Minimizing geopolitical and currency risks through rigorous multi-jurisdictional trust and legal structures, ensuring absolute partner asset safety.

Pillar 02

資產重組活化 Asset Realignment

專注於價值低估、重組潛力巨大的企業股權與實體資產。藉由債權重組與管理優化，釋放被鎖定的核心資產價值。 Sourcing undervalued assets and corporate entities to unlock intrinsic value, restructuring debt, and optimizing operational efficiency.

Pillar 03

機構協同效應 Institutional Synergy

與全球主權基金、家族辦公室及頂尖金融機構協同合作，建構兼具隱密性與靈活度的股權配置管道。 Aligning directly with global sovereign wealth funds, family offices, and institutional partners to construct highly flexible and secure investments.

核心運作 Core Operations

我們針對每一筆資產的收購與注資，皆遵循頂級機構的 4 階段標準運作流程（SOP），以科學、嚴謹的框架保障投資目標的順利達成。 We follow a strict 4-phase Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) for asset acquisition, restructuring, and equity injection, providing a robust risk-insulated framework.

I

Acquisition & Origination

階段一：資產篩選與估值 Stage 1: Asset Sourcing & Evaluation

利用全球合作網絡，主動發掘處於特殊狀況、價值被低估或面臨跨境架構調整需求的優質實體資產與股權。經由內部專家小組初步篩選後，進行基於現金流與清算價值的評估。 Rigorous identification of undervalued, distressed, or operationally bottlenecked assets. We apply strict proprietary filters to isolate assets with significant structural upside.

II

Due Diligence & Structuring

階段二：架構設計與深度審查 Stage 2: Structuring & Audits

啟動法務、財務、稅務及跨境監管合規的全面盡職調查。針對資產特性設計精準的持股架構與風險隔離機制，以確保交易結構在稅務效益與合規性上達到最優配置。 Exhaustive legal, fiscal, and operational audits. Our multi-jurisdictional experts structure tax-efficient, risk-insulated investment vehicles to safeguard partner capital.

III

Capital Injection & Revitalization

階段三：私募注資與營運活化 Stage 3: Equity Injection & Revitalization

執行股權收購或結構性債資注入，提供資產重整所需的流動性。同時，派駐核心重組團隊介入資產營運或改組董事會，實施降本增效、資產處置等重組措施，徹底活化資產效能。 Direct private equity deployment paired with operational restructuring. We overhaul management, streamline cost structures, and inject liquidity to revive asset efficiency.

IV

Value Realization & Exit

階段四：退出機制與價值實現 Stage 4: Exit & Realization

當資產營運與財務指標達到預期軌道後，透過跨國併購（M&A）、管理層回購（MBO）或特定機構私募轉讓等成熟管道實施股權退出，為合作之機構投資人實現溢價回報與資本套現。 Executing planned exits through mergers & acquisitions (M&A), management buyouts (MBO), or structured secondary market transactions, delivering optimized returns.
